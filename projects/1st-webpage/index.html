About Me

Initially raised eight years in Ghana. Educated in England, soon after, a spell in the military. Studied furniture design and crafts (traditional cabinetmaker). Followed by too many years as a sales representative for a timber importer.

Plenty other jobs in between: hotel porter, barman, beach-litter-picker, shelf stacker, motorcycle courier, the list goes on and on and on!

I've backpacked ten months round the world. Hong Kong, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Australia, New Zealand, Fiji, America, Canada, and back out through New York.

Then a new direction: trained as a commercial pilot in South Africa. I flew safaris in Namibia and Botswana, with a brief spell in Tanzania.

Next, I spent time (still am cycle touring) as a cycle adventurer round New Zealand and up through Tasmania. Between cycling, I managed a 'Backpackers' on the south island of New Zealand for a summer. Returning to England and completed a cycle around the rugged coast of my home county of Cornwall. Then enjoyed a glorious summers cycling up through Wales. nigelsbicyclejourneys.com *

Now, I'm embarking on yet another adventure: to pursue a career as a 'Web Programmer'. And, the difference is: I'm starting mid-life! Enthusiasm = Learning!

I'm fully aware that I'll need to work on a ‘portfolio' of projects. As relying on just training, will not be sufficient. This is where my first webpage comes into play. Why not start that train, by showing ones progression as a project for that said portfolio. Watch this space!
