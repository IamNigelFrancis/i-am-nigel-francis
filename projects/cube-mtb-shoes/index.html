CUBE Shoes ALL MOUNTAIN

Shoe lasts are reproductions of the average human foot and the most important part of the manufacturing process as the lasts are what determine the shape and fit of the finished shoe. All CUBE lasts have been developed in line with the demands and considerations of the CUBE Natural Fit concept.

All products are GREAT... NO - AWESOME!

The CUBE ALL MOUNTAIN shoe has a classic lacing and a 45° velcro with Cube wing. The very flexible and non-slip outsole is made of a special rubber compound which allows also managing to carry or push the bike for a longer time period. Furthermore, the sole is rigid enough to distribute the power on the pedal.

Latest Blog Post

CUBE’s impressive new purpose built production facility has now begun producing bicycles, upping potential annual capacity by as much as 75% at peak times.

Located just across the road from the cube factorybrand’s former Waldershof production line, the new home covers some 800,000 square metres, housing for the first time a dedicated production line capable of handling the firm’s marked increase in electric bike SKUs.

Just three years ago the brand had three electric bikes. For MY17 some 104 SKUs make up a catalogue covering a variety of urban and off road uses. The firm’s e-bike department manager estimated that the portfolio now makes up around 30% of the entire catalogue, something which goes a long way to explaining a new on-site steep gradient test track.
